What is the Ultras Movement?

Ultras are passionate supporters' collectives found around the world, especially in football (soccer) culture. Born in Italy in the late 1960s, the movement quickly spread across Europe, North Africa, South America, and beyond.

Known for their colorful displays, relentless chants, and creative banners, ultras shape the atmosphere in stadiums and stand as defenders of their club, city, and values. The movement is rooted in principles of loyalty, resistance, solidarity, and pride.
